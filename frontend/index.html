About SRKR ISTE

SRKR ISTE (Indian Society for Technical Education) is a professional society committed to the overall development of technical education in India. We aim to provide students with a platform to enhance their technical skills, foster innovation, and promote research and development in various fields of technology.

Mission and Vision

Our Vision

Our vision is to be recognized as a center of excellence in technical education, empowering students with knowledge and skills that meet global industry standards. We strive to create an environment that encourages creativity, critical thinking, and lifelong learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide world-class technical education that nurtures students' talents, instills values, and prepares them to take on challenges in the ever-changing technological landscape. We are dedicated to producing socially responsible and innovative engineers who contribute to the betterment of society.
